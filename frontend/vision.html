Futuristic Vision — Telemonitoring & SaaS for Neuro Research

From an educational Dementia demo to a cloud platform for continuous monitoring, longitudinal research, and collaborative neuroscience tools.

1. Alzheimer’s Disease Telemonitoring

Remote, frequent evaluation of voice and remote sensor biomarkers to track disease progression and therapy response. Think weekly voice checks, automated trend reports, and clinician alerts on significant change.

2. Multi-modal data

Beyond voice: integrate wearable motor metrics, touchscreen tapping tests, and patient-reported scales. All fused into time-series models for early warning and research-grade phenotyping.

3. SaaS for researchers

Secure cloud platform where labs upload data, run validated pipelines, and share reproducible experiments. Multi-tenant, role-based access, and exportable datasets for clinical studies.

Ethics & privacy

Health data protections, opt-in consent, and anonymized research exports. The demo is educational; production requires full regulatory and privacy compliance.

Roadmap (next 3 years)
Prototype → Pilot studies → SaaS MVP → Clinical partnerships
  • Year 1 — robust telemetric data collection + regulatory checklist.
  • Year 2 — federated analytics, cohort dashboards, and clinician UI.
  • Year 3 — SaaS platform, institutional pilots, and academic integrations.
